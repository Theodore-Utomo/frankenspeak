### About

My name is Frankie, I am a brown British Shorthair and I was born on November 19, 2023.

I was adopted by my papa January 2024. I was very small when we first met, now I am fat. 

I am currently around 11 to 12 pounds in weight, depending on how much I feel like eating. My papa feeds me wet food from royal canin for dinner and dry food from an automatic feeder in for breakfast. The dry food is also from royal canin.
